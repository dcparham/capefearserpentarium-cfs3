Our Location

We are located where Orange Street meets the Cape Fear River in historic downtown Wilmington, North Carolina (between River and Front Streets). Enjoy the scenic atmosphere of horse-drawn carriages and trolleys while shopping and touring our neighboring attractions.

 

Enjoy the hospitality of Hampton without the hustle & bustle. Our Hampton Inn Wilmington - Eastwood, North Carolina hotel is situated halfway between the Atlantic Ocean and the historic Cape Fear River. Our guests can enjoy strolling on the Riverfront Walkway or walking white sandy beaches, all within a 5-mile ride in either direction. Choose from the Landfall location or Smith Creek Station.
