Group Rates and Special Tours
Hours and Group Rates

The Serpentarium is open from 11AM to 5PM on weekdays, 11AM to 6PM Saturdays and Sundays. Hours may vary seasonally, so please phone for confirmation. All tickets are $9.00. Children under 2 years of age are free. Click here to find out about special group rates, class field trips, narrated educational tours and birthday parties with the chance to get up close and personal with a real live friendly snake.

Book your special group tour at the discounted rate of $7.00 per person for any group of 15 or more. Group tours that are booked in advance include the special attention of a zookeeper for a narrated tour and some hands-on interaction time with one of our museum's friendliest snakes. Narrated tours are given by age and interest level. A 25% deposit for your estimated number of guests is requested to reserve your group. Click here to find out about hosting your child's birthday party at the Serpentarium.

Kids and grownups alike will enjoy the thrill of touching and holding a real live snake. On weekends at 3PM and at other times for specially arranged group tours, an exciting feeding show lets you watch from just a few feet away as our animals jump, bite, slither or strike for their food.
